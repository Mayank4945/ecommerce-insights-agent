## üìπ Recording Tips

### Video Quality
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 (1080p) minimum
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps
- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Bitrate**: 5-8 Mbps for good quality

### Audio Quality
- **Microphone**: Use a decent mic (headset or USB mic)
- **Environment**: Quiet room, minimal background noise
- **Volume**: Consistent, not too loud or soft
- **Pace**: Moderate speed, clear pronunciation

### Screen Recording
- **Tool Options**:
  - OBS Studio (free, powerful)
  - Camtasia (paid, user-friendly)
  - Loom (free, web-based)
  - ShareX (free, Windows)
- **Settings**:
  - Record full screen or application window
  - Hide desktop icons and taskbar if possible
  - Close unnecessary applications
  - Disable notifications

### Presentation
- **Cursor**: Use cursor highlighting
- **Zoom**: Zoom in on important parts
- **Pacing**: Don't rush, pause between sections
- **Energy**: Be enthusiastic but professional
- **Script**: Have notes but don't read verbatim
